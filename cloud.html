About

A cloud kitchen, also known as a ghost kitchen or virtual kitchen, is a commercial kitchen designed specifically for food delivery services. Unlike traditional restaurants, cloud kitchens do not have a dine-in facility, allowing businesses to focus solely on online orders through food delivery platforms
